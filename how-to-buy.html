How to Buy $NXR

A complete step-by-step guide to purchasing $NXR token on Base Network. Takes less than 5 minutes if you already have a wallet.

⏱  Estimated Time: ~5 Minutes
⚠️

Important: Always verify the official contract address before swapping. Never trust links from unofficial sources. The only official $NXR contract address is listed below. Always do your own research (DYOR) before investing in any cryptocurrency.

01
Set Up Your Wallet
⏱ 2 minutes — one-time setup

You'll need a Web3 wallet that supports Base Network. We recommend MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet — both are free and easy to set up.

  • aDownload MetaMask from metamask.io (browser extension or mobile app) or Coinbase Wallet from your app store
  • bCreate a new wallet and securely back up your seed phrase — write it down on paper, never share it with anyone
  • cAdd Base Network to your wallet using chainlist.org — search for "Base" and click "Add to MetaMask"

02
Get ETH on Base Network
⏱ 1–3 minutes

You need ETH on Base Network to swap for $NXR (and to pay small gas fees). There are two ways to get ETH on Base:

  • aOption A — Bridge from Ethereum: Use the official Base Bridge to transfer ETH from Ethereum mainnet to Base. Takes ~2 minutes.
  • bOption B — Buy directly on Base: Some exchanges (Coinbase, Binance) allow you to withdraw ETH directly to Base Network. Check withdrawal options.
  • cOption C — Third-party bridges: Use Orbiter Finance, Stargate, or Across Protocol for fast, cheap bridging from other L2s like Arbitrum or Optimism.

03
Swap ETH → $NXR on Uniswap
⏱ 1 minute

Now you're ready to buy $NXR. Use Uniswap (the largest DEX on Base) to swap your ETH for $NXR tokens.

  • aGo to app.uniswap.org and connect your wallet
  • bMake sure you're on Base Network (check the network selector in the top right)
  • cSet the "From" token to ETH and the "To" token to $NXR
  • dPaste the official $NXR contract address (below) to import the token
  • eEnter the amount of ETH you want to swap, review the details, and click "Swap"
  • fConfirm the transaction in your wallet — done! 🎉

04
Add $NXR to Your Wallet & Hold
⏱ 30 seconds

After the swap is complete, you'll have $NXR tokens in your wallet. To see them in your wallet interface:

  • aIn MetaMask, scroll down and click "Import Tokens"
  • bPaste the $NXR contract address and the token should auto-fill (Name: Nexora, Symbol: NXR, Decimals: 18)
  • cClick "Add Custom Token" and confirm — your $NXR balance will now be visible!

🎉 Congratulations! You now hold $NXR. Start exploring the Nexora tools and join our community!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is slippage and how much should I set? ▼

Slippage is the maximum price difference you'll accept between when you submit a swap and when it executes. For $NXR (0% tax), a slippage of 0.5% - 1% is usually sufficient. If the swap fails, try increasing to 2-3%.

How much are gas fees on Base? ▼

Base is an Ethereum Layer 2, so gas fees are extremely low — typically less than $0.01 per transaction. You can check current gas prices on our Gas Tracker tool.

Is there a buy or sell tax on $NXR? ▼

No. $NXR has 0% buy tax and 0% sell tax. What you see is what you get — no hidden fees or token deductions on any transaction.

Can I buy $NXR on a centralized exchange (CEX)? ▼

Currently, $NXR is only available on decentralized exchanges (DEX) via Uniswap on Base Network. CEX listings will be announced via our official channels.

My swap is failing — what should I do? ▼

Try increasing slippage to 2-3%. Make sure you have enough ETH for gas fees (keep at least 0.001 ETH aside). If using MetaMask, try clearing activity data in Settings → Advanced → Clear Activity Tab Data.
