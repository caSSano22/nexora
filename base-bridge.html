🌉 How to Bridge (Step-by-Step)

  1. 01

    Connect Your Wallet

    Visit bridge.base.org (or your chosen bridge). Click "Connect Wallet" and approve the connection in MetaMask or Coinbase Wallet. Make sure you have ETH on the source chain to pay for gas.

  2. 02

    Select Networks & Token

    Set the From network (e.g., Ethereum Mainnet) and To network (Base). Select the token you want to bridge — usually ETH. Enter the amount to bridge.

  3. 03

    Review & Confirm

    Review the bridge details: amount, estimated fees, and expected arrival time. Click "Bridge" or "Transfer". Confirm the transaction in your wallet. You'll pay a gas fee on the source chain.

  4. 04

    Wait & Receive

    The bridge will process your transfer. Official Base Bridge takes ~2 minutes. Third-party bridges can be as fast as 15 seconds. Once complete, your ETH will appear in your wallet on Base Network. Switch to Base Network in MetaMask to see your balance.

💡

Pro Tip: If you're bridging from another L2 (like Arbitrum or Optimism), use Orbiter Finance or Across Protocol instead of the official Base Bridge. The official bridge only supports Ethereum L1 → Base. Third-party bridges support L2 → L2 transfers which are faster and cheaper.
