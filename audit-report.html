🔎 Detailed Findings

F-01 — Top Holder Concentration (Medium)

The top 10 wallet addresses collectively hold more than 40% of the total $NXR supply. While the team allocation (15%) is vested and the liquidity pool (50%) is locked, large holder concentration presents a theoretical sell-pressure risk.

Team Response: Acknowledged. The team wallet is subject to a 6-month vesting schedule with monthly linear unlock. Community allocation will be distributed through airdrops, rewards, and incentive programs to increase holder diversity over time.

F-02 — Zero-Value Transfer Events (Informational)

The contract does not emit a Transfer event when a zero-value transfer is executed. While this does not pose a security risk, some indexing services and wallets may not correctly track zero-value interactions.

Resolution: Fixed in contract update. Transfer events are now emitted for all transfers regardless of value.

F-03 — EIP-2612 Permit (Informational)

The contract does not implement EIP-2612 (permit) functionality which allows gasless approvals via signed messages. While not a security concern, adding this feature would improve DeFi composability.

Team Response: Acknowledged. Permit functionality will be considered for future contract iterations.
